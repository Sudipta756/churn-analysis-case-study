# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis

## 📌 Overview

This project analyzes customer churn to uncover patterns and key factors influencing customer attrition. The goal is to help businesses improve retention strategies by leveraging data-driven insights.

Customer churn is a major concern across industries, as acquiring new customers is significantly more expensive than retaining existing ones. This project provides a structured approach to understanding and mitigating churn.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Identify key drivers of customer churn
* Analyze behavioral and financial patterns of customers
* Provide actionable insights to reduce churn rates

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset contains customer-level information, including:

* **Demographics**: Gender, Senior Citizen, etc.
* **Account Information**: Tenure, Contract Type
* **Services**: Internet, Phone, Streaming Services
* **Billing Details**: Monthly Charges, Total Charges
* **Target Variable**: Churn (Yes/No)

---

### 3. Feature Analysis

* Evaluated impact of:

  * Tenure

  * Monthly Charges

### 4. Insights Extraction

* Derived meaningful business insights from patterns

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* Customers with **month-to-month contracts** show higher churn rates
* **Low tenure customers** are more likely to leave
* **Higher monthly charges** correlate with increased churn
* Customers with fewer subscribed services tend to churn more

---

## 📈 Business Recommendations

* Encourage long-term contracts with incentives
* Improve onboarding experience for new customers
* Offer personalized pricing or discounts
* Increase engagement through bundled services
